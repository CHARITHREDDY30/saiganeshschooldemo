How It Began

A Dream Planted
in Humble Soil

Sri Sai Ganesh Vidyalayam was founded in 1998 by Shri Ramakrishna Rao, a retired educator with a singular vision: to create a school in the neighbourhood that combined rigorous academics with genuine care for every child.

What began as a small institution with 120 students and 8 teachers has grown into a thriving community of over 1,800 students across 14 classes, led by more than 80 trained educators and staff.

Where We Are Today

A Legacy Built on
Trust & Results

Today, Sri Sai Ganesh Vidyalayam is one of the most trusted CBSE schools in the region. Our students have gone on to prestigious institutions across India and abroad, excelling in medicine, engineering, arts, and public service.

Our alumni network of over 1,200 graduates stands as the most powerful testament to our commitment — parents who themselves studied here now proudly enroll their own children.

Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped Us

1998
School Founded
Opened with 120 students and 8 teachers in a rented two-storey building in Vidya Nagar, Hyderabad.
2003
CBSE Affiliation Secured
Sri Sai Ganesh Vidyalayam received official affiliation from CBSE, New Delhi, enabling Classes up to X.
2008
New Campus Inauguration
Moved to the current purpose-built campus with dedicated science labs, a library, and sports grounds.
2012
Senior Secondary Wing Launched
Introduced Classes XI and XII with Science and Commerce streams, completing the full school cycle.
2018
Digital Classrooms Across Campus
Completed full rollout of smart boards and digital learning infrastructure in all 48 classrooms.
2023
Silver Jubilee Celebrations
Celebrated 25 years of excellence with a grand alumni reunion, felicitating graduates who made the institution proud.
