How to Apply

Our Admission Process

1

Fill out the online inquiry form on the right or visit the school office. Basic details about your child and preferred class are required. No fee at this stage.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there an entrance exam for admission?

For Nursery to Class I, there is no formal exam. For Class II onwards, a friendly informal interaction with our teachers assesses the child's readiness — not to screen them out, but to place them appropriately.

Can I visit the school before applying?

Absolutely. We hold Open Days every month and also welcome scheduled visits throughout the week. Call our admissions office at +91 98765 43210 to book a tour.

Are there scholarships or fee concessions?

Yes. We offer concessions for SC/ST/OBC students, siblings of existing students, and children of teaching staff. Merit scholarships are available for students entering Class IX with outstanding results.

What is the medium of instruction?

The primary medium of instruction is English. Telugu is offered as the second language. Hindi is compulsory as the third language up to Class VIII, as per CBSE norms.

Is transport facility available?

Yes, we operate school buses covering major areas of the city. Transport fees are charged separately. Please contact the school office for route and availability details.

What is the refund policy if we withdraw?

The registration fee is non-refundable. Admission fee is refundable up to 30 days before the academic session begins, subject to a processing deduction as per school policy.
